# 📊 Sales & Customer Performance Dashboard | Tableau

An interactive **Tableau Dashboard** built using the **Sample Superstore** dataset to analyze **Sales Performance** and **Customer Behavior**. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify trends, and make data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project consists of two interactive dashboards:

- 📈 Sales Performance Dashboard
- 👥 Customer Performance Dashboard

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze Year-over-Year (YoY) sales performance.
- Track sales, profit, and order trends.
- Compare product category and subcategory performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify high-value customers.
- Enable interactive business analysis through filters and navigation.

---

## 📈 Sales Dashboard

### KPI Overview

- Total Sales (Current Year vs Previous Year)
- Total Profit (Current Year vs Previous Year)
- Total Quantity Sold (Current Year vs Previous Year)

### Sales Trends

- Monthly Sales Trend
- Monthly Profit Trend
- Monthly Quantity Trend
- Highlights the highest and lowest performing months

### Product Subcategory Analysis

- Compare Sales by Product Subcategory
- Compare Profit by Product Subcategory
- Year-over-Year comparison

### Weekly Sales & Profit Analysis

- Weekly Sales and Profit
- Average Weekly Sales
- Average Weekly Profit
- Highlights weeks performing above and below the average

---

## 👥 Customer Dashboard

### KPI Overview

- Total Customers
- Sales per Customer
- Total Orders
- Current Year vs Previous Year comparison

### Customer Trends

- Monthly Customer Growth
- Monthly Sales per Customer
- Monthly Orders
- Highlights the highest and lowest performing months

### Customer Distribution

- Distribution of customers based on the number of orders placed
- Provides insights into customer loyalty and purchasing behavior

### Top 10 Customers

Displays:

- Customer Rank
- Customer Name
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Number of Orders
- Last Order Date
